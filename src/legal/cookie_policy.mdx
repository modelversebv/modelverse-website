## 1. Introduction

Modelverse B.V. respects your privacy and is committed to transparency about how cookies are used on our website.

## 2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and provide information to site owners.

## 3. Our Use of Cookies

We do **not** use cookies ourselves to collect personal data or track visitors. However, our website includes embedded third-party services that may use cookies:

- **Google Maps** (for location features)
- **YouTube** (for embedded videos)

These third-party services may place cookies on your device and collect data according to their own policies.

## 4. Third-Party Cookies

We have no control over the cookies set by third parties. For more information on how these third parties handle your data, please refer to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## 5. Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of embedded content such as maps and videos.
